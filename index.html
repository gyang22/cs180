Project 1: Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii photo collection

In this project, we created algorithms to transform the Prokudin-Gorskii glass plate negatives taken of various images in separate R, G, and B channels to color images. The basic algorithm involves cutting the image into thirds, then iterating over various offsets of the image to compare alignment similarity scores, then upscaling the resolution to search again with more precision.
